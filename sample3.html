Materials

All staple DNA, anchor oligo DNA, glue DNA and gold nanoparticle were purchased from Sigma-Aldrich Japan.
Single-stranded M13mp18 DNA were purchased from BioLabs Inc.

Experiment Methods

Method Ⅰ---Synthesis of origami rectangle (1),(3)

  • Dissolve each staple strands in 1×TAE buffer and prepare 0.2mM staple solution.
  • Mix all staple solution (2μl of each) to prepare staple-mix solution.
  • Mix reagents for PCR as following order in micro tube;
    • 　　・Milli Q 7.8μl
    　　・10×TAE 2μl
    　　・50mM Magnesium Acetate 5μl
    　　・Staple DNA solution 1.2μl
    　　・Scaffold DNA solution 4μl
  • Anneal sample as following conditions
    • 　　95℃ 5min
    　　↓
    　　95℃→20℃　gradually lower the annealing temperature
    　　 ( 1℃/min in 0.1℃ steps)
  • Resulting solution contains DNA origami rectangles.
    •

    MethodⅡ---Preparation of AuNP-anchor oligo DNA conjugates (2)

  • Dissolve SH-modified ahchor oligo DNA in 0.072M DTT for 1h at room temperature to remove the protective group.
  • Purify anchor oligo DNA by using NAP-5 column.
  • Dilute resulting solution with 10mM Tris-HCl pH7.0 and measure Abs at 260 nm. (0.7Abs)
  • Calculate the concentration by using the formulas below.
    • 0.7(Abs)×0.85×5=(2.975 OD)⁄850μl= (3.5 OD)⁄(1000μl ) 3.5OD⁄1000μl×35.1μg⁄OD=( 122.85μg)⁄ml →0.0317×〖10〗^(-6) mol/ml→0.0317×106 nM

  • Concentration of AuNP (diemeter:5nm) solution ; 5.5×106 nano-particles /liter

  • Concentration of purified anchor oligo DNA solution ; 0.317×106 nM
  • Mix 1ml AuNP solution and 94.64μl anchor oligo DNA solution (contains 3nmol oligo).
  • Rotate the solution 16h at room temperature at low speed.
  • Add 1M NaCl and 0.1M sodium phosphate buffer (137μl of each) to the solution.

    • Final concentration ; 0.1M NaCl 10mM phosphate buffer

  • Rotate the solution 24h at room temperature at low speed.
  • Centrifuge 60min at 45,000g.
  • Remove the supernatant and resuspend the red oil in the same volume of 0.1M NaCl/10mM sodium phosphate buffer.
  • Centrifuge 60min at 45,000g.
  • Remove the supernatant again and resuspend the red oil in the same volume of 0.3M NaCl/0.01% sodium azide/10mM sodium phosphate buffer.
  • Measure Absorbance at 520nm. (0.26Abs)
  • Calculate the concentration of the conjugate solution by using folulas below.
    •
    C_n=(A_520×1)(〖10〗^7×b) b ; path length of the cuvette (typically 1cm) Cn=(0.26(Abs)×1)/(107×0.5)=0.52×107 M= 52nM


    MethodⅢ---Attaching AuNP on the surface of DNA origami and colum purification

  • Mix 15μl DNA origami solution and 172.5μl AuNP-oligo solution and leave 1h at room temperature.
  • Purify solution to remove excess staples and AuNP-oligo by using Sephacryl S-300HR (GE Heaithcare). (3)
    •

    MethodⅣ---AFM observation (3)

  • Before AFM, add 2μl glue DNA solution to 98μl purified DNA origami with AuNP solution and leave it more than 1h.
  • 1μl sample were adsorbed on the mica sheet for 5min at room temperature and then add 50μl 1×TAE buffer.
  • Get AFM images using Multimode 8 (Bruker).
  • AFM probes ; SCANASYST-FLUID (Nitride Coated Silicon Tip on a Nitride Cantilever), SCANASYST-FLUID＋(Silicon Tip on Nitride Lever
